🔥 "Life-Stage Navigator"

Consumers approaching major milestones — first home, new business, retirement — want proactive guidance, not product pitches. 73% said their bank never reached out before a life event. FCCB's relationship model is perfectly positioned here but lacks a trigger system. Est. 2,400 new mortgage/loan conversations per year.

⚡ "Small Business After-Hours"

Small business owners do financial management after 8pm. They need real-time cash flow visibility and next-day deposit confirmation. 61% say their bank is "asleep" when they work. Est. 18% deposit growth from improved digital cash management tools.

🎓 "Financial Confidence Builder"

Younger customers want to feel smart about money, not sold to. They gravitate toward banks that educate — FCCB Academy is an untapped differentiator that fewer than 12% of prospects are aware of. Est. 35% increase in Student/Budget Checking acquisition with promotion.

Borrower Archetypes
🔍 Rate Hunters (31%)

Compare 5+ lenders. Use Bankrate and NerdWallet obsessively. Will sacrifice relationship for 0.125% lower rate. Hardest to win but high-value if cross-sold.

🤝 Relationship Seekers (28%)

Want a named human who answers the phone. Will pay slightly more for local, in-house servicing. FCCB's sweet spot — they just don't know FCCB offers this.

😰 Overwhelmed First-Timers (24%)

Paralyzed by complexity. Don't understand PMI, escrow, or DTI. Gravitate toward whoever simplifies first. FCCB's first-time buyer grants are nearly unknown in this group.

⚡ Speed Optimizers (17%)

Experienced buyers who want fast pre-approval and minimal paperwork. Over-index on Rocket Mortgage and Better.com. Will abandon any process that takes more than 48 hours to respond.
